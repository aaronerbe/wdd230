The Idaho City Chamber of Commerce

Welcome to the Idaho City Chamber of Commerce.

We are your community's bridge to prosperity and growth. As a dynamic and forward-thinking organization, we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant business environment, driving economic development, and enhancing the quality of life for all residents in our thriving city/region.

Our Chamber together a diverse network of businesses, entrepreneurs, and community leaders, all working collaboratively to create opportunities, advocate for businesses, and facilitate connections. Whether you're a long-standing business, a start-up, or an individual looking to get involved, we invite you to explore the countless benefits and resources that Idaho City Chamber of Commerce has to offer.
