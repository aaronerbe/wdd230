Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Aaron Erbe

Site Name

Idaho City Chamber of Commerce

Name represents the City and clearly identifies the purpose of the website

Purpose

The Idaho City Chamber of Commerce serves as an advocate for a prosperous economy and a stronger community. We serve our members through business promotion, education, community engagement and advocacy. We currently serve 30+ business in the Idaho City area.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a directory of business in the area including contact information, business hours and services they provide?
  • What community events are coming up? Who are the sponsors? Where and when will they be? How can a business get involved?
  • How can a business join the chamber of commerce and what services or benefits can the business expect?
  • Where can I find information about the demographics and devleopment of the area?
  • Where can I find information on local government policies and regulations impacting business?
  • How can I contact Chamber of Commerce staff?
  • How can I follow through social media?
  • What are the rewards and recognition of outstanding businesses in the area?
