Explore Cozumel

Snorkeling

Cozumel is a premier destination for snorkeling and scuby diving. Tours, equipment and classes are all avaialble at any of our excellent partners. Expect to see sea horses, eagle rays and turtles. For best snorkeling, we recommend the west coast beaches. The beaches are more limestone and less sand making for higher visibility and coral growth.

Mayan Ruins

Cozumel is rich with Mayan history. If you have the time, we highliy recommend visiting Chichén Itzá, Mexico to see one of the 7 Wonders of the World. If you need to stay on our beautiful island, we recommend visiting the San Gervasio site.

Beaches

What better way to explore our beaches than in one of our Jeeps. Nothing beats a beach bonfire and sunset. All beaches in Mexico are public. However, some require a fee to access the facilities.
